Welcome to your Pill Box!

Your Pillbox is where you will enter information about your medication.

  1. Step 1: Identify the pill slot on the machine where you wish to store your medication.
  2. Step 2: Pour medication into the selected slot.
  3. Step 3: On the Pill Box Page, click on the slot where you stored your medication and fill out the form with the corresponding medication information.
  4. Step 4: Navigate to the dispensing page to dispense medication when ready!
